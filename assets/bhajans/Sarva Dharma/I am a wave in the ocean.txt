I am a wave in the ocean
With every breeze I rise and fall 
I am a snowdrop swirling wildly 
Every breeze throws me around 

Must I forever be a wave Lord
Or a snowdrop in a storm 
Let me forever be the ocean 
Let no wind throw me around 

Hare Rama Hare Krishna 
Sathya Sai Bhagawan 
O My Lord Dear Jesus 
Buddha Nanak Dear Tuhan

Peace within is my goal Lord 
Never throw me up and down 
And in the end when I leave Lord
Let You and I merge as one
