Yasni svet garit v’dushe,
eto thi prishol ka mne,
gaspoyt’ Sai Baba
Mir sagrel svayim teplom,
nam atkril duhovne dom,
gaspoyt’ Sai Baba
Y pafsyudu na puti
del blagyh tvayih slidi,
gaspoyt’ Sai Baba
Eto hramov kupola,
eto nebo y zimlya,
gaspoyt’ Sai Baba
Thi prihodish k’nam va snah
y Iisus nash y Alah,
gaspoyt’ Sai Baba
Darish vsyem lyubov’ svayu,
dlya tebya syertsa payut,
gaspoyt’ Sai Baba
