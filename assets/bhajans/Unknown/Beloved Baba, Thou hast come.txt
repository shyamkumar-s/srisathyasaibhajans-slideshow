Beloved Baba, Thou hast come
For the Celestial Light to shine
 In darkest corners of our hearts
 And fill us with Thy Love divine
 When ignorance, jealously, hate and pride
 Dissolve away into the air
 Then free at last we learn to see that
 God alone was ever there.
 So open now our sleeping eyes that
 Through these windows of the soul,
 Thy love may flow to show the way
 We all must go to reach the goal
