The Light Meditation
So the light shines throughout the universe,&nbsp;
Spreading rays of love and peace and understanding,&nbsp;
That will help unite the brotherhood of man.&nbsp;
I am in the light, the light is in me, I am the light. (x2)&nbsp;

Let these eyes see the good in those around me,&nbsp;
And these ears listen only to what is true.&nbsp;
May this tongue of mine speak in moderation,&nbsp;
Words of wisdom that are full of sweetness too.&nbsp;
I am in the light, the light is in me, I am the light. (x2)&nbsp;

These two hands of mine must strive to serve my brothers,&nbsp;
And these feet should help me do what is right.&nbsp;
With a heart full of love and compassion,&nbsp;
See the world and all its beings bathed in light.
I am in the light, the light is in me, I am the light. (x2)
