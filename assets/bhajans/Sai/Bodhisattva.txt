Bodhisattva
Praise God, you’ve taken birth
Bodhisattva, you’ve come again
Selfless servant, you’re the Lord’s true friend.
 
Hands that heal and a heart so pure
A peace so real and a love so sure
A life of service to the One
Precious Soul you’re a shining sun. [ Bodhisattva on this earth ... ]
 
From the morning ‘till the night
Everywhere you spread your light
With each breath you live for Him
God’s own heart beats within.
