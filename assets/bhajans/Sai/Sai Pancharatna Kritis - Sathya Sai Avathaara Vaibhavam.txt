Sai Pancharatna Kritis - Sathya Sai Avathaara Vaibhavam
(Pallavi)

Sathya Dharma Shanthi Prema
Prathihrudhi Nilipina Vishwaprema Prasthaanam
(Anupallavi)

Prema Ekaika Matham Sai Bodhaamrutha Saaram
(Charanam)

1. Udaattha Maanaveeya Sevaye Aadhyaathmika Saadhana Tholi Sopaanam

2. Graama Sevamari, Deena Jana Sevaye Karma Yogam Chakkani Raaja Maargam

3. Naada Brahma Sai Sankeerthaname Kalilo Mukthi Maargam

4. Bhaava Raaga Thaala Melana, Bhajanaanandame Bhakthi Maargam

5. Soham, Soham, Soham Yerukaye Jnaanam              
Anuvanuvuna Ninnu Kanugonute Jnaana Maargam

6. Kalushitha Kali Maanava Medhaahamkruthi Doorikaranam
Kula Matha Deshaatheeta Vishwajaneena,
Sathyapathanirdheshanam Saayi Mahitodhyamam

7. Premaakarshana Shakthiyutha Sayee    
Janaganamanamula Divyathwa Jaagruthiki Velasina Vishwa Viraat Roopaa
Nithya, Shuddha, Buddha, Chaitanya Deepthaa Apuroopa Leelaavathaara

8. Sai Siddhaanthamulu Vannemeera Saakaaramaaye
Bhuvi Dharmakshetram, Jagathi Vasudaika Kutumbam
Digdhigantha Dheedhithula Thejarille
Sathya Sai Vibhavam Apoorvam Akhandam Anantham
