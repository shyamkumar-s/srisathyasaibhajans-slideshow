I Love You So, Baba
Be in my heart, wherever I go,
Grant me Thy wisdom, that I may know,
Sathya Sai.

Help me to see, Baba, help me to see,
Follow Thy path, a true devotee,
All that I am, I offer to Thee,
Sathya Sai.

Make me Thine own, Baba, make me Thine own,
In all my life, let Thy will be done,
Till the last day, when all will be one,
Sathya Sai.
