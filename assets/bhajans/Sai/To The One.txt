To The One
Baba my Lord, when I feel there's one to love me
I see Your eyes full of compassion and love looking at me
When I feel all alone and there’s no one to help me
I find Your hands everywhere ready to hold and uplift me
When I feel lonely and dejected my Lord, Your eyes show You're the one who cares for me
Just a cry from my heart, a prayer upon my soul, dearest Lord never ever leave me
