Sai Pancharatna Kritis - Sadaa Bhaavayaami
Sai Paripoornaavataaram
(Pallavi)

Sadaa Naadopaasita Modam
Maayaamaanusha Vesha Dhaaram
(Anupallavi)

Prema Thanumanisham Karunaa Hrudayam
(Charanam)

1. Veda Purusha Veda Poshakam
Veda Saara Roopa Veda Gaanalolam

2. Lingodbhava Bhasmaabhisheka Leelaa Vilaasa Parthi Shivam

3. Nenundaga Bhayamelananuchu Abhayamosage Anugraha Vrushti

4. Bhakthaapa Mruthyu Haranam Gathajeeva Prana Pradaayakam

5. Poovu Puttagane Parimalinchu Reethi Baala
Praayambuna Tyaaga Sheelatha nerpe

6. Nee Avathaara Paramaarthamunu Sunthaina Theliyudure
Nirvyaaja Premaathishayamutho Ninnu Neeveruka Parachani Naadu

7. Purushulalona Purushudavu Pasivaarilona Baaludavu
Mahilala Madhya Mahilavu Manaserigi Manoradhamuleederchu
Prana Vibhudavu Neevu Eakaanthambuna Vishwavibhudaveevu

8. Yuvathaku Anusaraneeyam Sathyam Samaya Paalanam
Hitha Mitha Mrudu Bhaashanam Samuchitha Veshadhaaranam
Prathi Panilo Nindaina Samskaaram Seva Paraayanathvam
Sathyam Nithya Vratham Saayi Jeevithame Jagathiki Sandesham

9. Deena Jana Sevaye Saayiki Santhushti
Sarva Jeeva Raasulapaina Kaarunya Drushti
Janabhaagadheya Easwaraamba Thanayaa
Mee Kanusannala Saagu Ee Sarva Srushti
