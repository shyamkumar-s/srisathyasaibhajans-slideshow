Sai Pancharatna Kritis - Kaliyuga Avathaari
(Pallavi)

Paalithajana Mangalakaari Paavana Chitraa Thata Vihaari
(Anupallavi)

Prema Sudhaapooram Sevaa Soundarya Bhaasitham 
(Charanam)

1. Darahaasa Vilasitha Vadana Sarojam
Kutilakunthala Viraajamaanam

2. Nee Mrudhulaadhara Nirgalitha Vaaksudhalu Groli
Mari Mari Thalachi Thalachi Pulakinchi
Mukthamathulayye Suptha Chitthamulu

3. Kalayo Vaishnavamaayayo
Sathya Sankalpa Paramaardhamo
Parulu Parule Kaadhu Paramaathmaroopulana
Bruhath Sevaa Pradaayakaa

4. Ganga Jatadhara Kataaksha Veekshanamuna Upponge Sai Ganga 
Sujala Pathakamanaga Daahaarthula Aarthi Deerpanga

5. Divya Bhishagvarudu Adbhutha Bhavaroga Nivaarana Samardhudu
Pedala Aatmabandhuvai Velayinche Ghana Aarogya Dhaamam

6. hyaaga Yogamule Punaadhi, Sathseelame Paramaavadhi
Padhaardha, Paraardha Yekeekrutha Ihaparajnaana Vaaradhi
Nihshulka Bodhanaankitha Vishishta Vidyaalayamidhi

7. Sai Divya Darshanam Ghanapaapanaashanam
Divyapaada Sparshanam Karmamochanam
Sathya Sai Sankeerthanam Aathmaanu Sandhaanam
