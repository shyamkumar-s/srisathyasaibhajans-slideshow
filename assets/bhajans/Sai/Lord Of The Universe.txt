Lord Of The Universe
Mother of miracles, may Your love shine
We who adore You, worship Your name
Light of creation, heavenly flame.
 
Every soul who yearns to know You
Sings a song their very own
Every heart Your music flows through
Shall never be alone.
 
Lord of the Universe, radiant sun
God of compassion, merciful One
All forgiving, faithful friend
Guide our footsteps once again.
 
Like restless rivers searching
For the source where we have come
We long to feel Your ocean
Sweet Lord, carry us home.
 
Lord of the universe, Master of light
Long have we waited for Your precious sight
Prophets and sages let it be known
Every nation will kneel by Your throne.
 
Everywhere Your signs are written
On the earth and in the skies
Now we feel Your omnipresence
Your love before our eyes.
 
[ Lord of the universe ... heavenly flame ]
