Reveal Yourself
Sometimes as a husband, Lord, sometimes as a wife,
As a child, I held Your hand and learned to walk,
As mother and as father, Lord, I taught You how to talk,
Lord, I’ve been a traveler following a star,
A soldier and a pilgrim, Lord, I’ve sought You near and far,
Oh Lord, my name is Everyman, my face is every face,
And in this sacred silence, Lord, I ask You for Your grace.
Reveal Yourself, reveal Yourself to me,
Reveal Yourself as the one reality,
Show me Your divinity in everything I see,
And show me You’re the light inside of me.
And now I stand before You, Lord, at last,
Letting go my future, Lord, and letting go my past,
And in the end I know that from the start,
The one that I’ve been searching for is right inside my heart,
Lord, I’ve been a traveler following a star,
A soldier and a pilgrim, Lord, I’ve sought You near and far,
Oh, Lord, my name is Everyman, my face is every face,
And in this sacred silence, Lord, I ask You for Your grace.
