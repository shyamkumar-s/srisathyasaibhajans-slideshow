Sathya Sai Bhagawan
Sarva jana priya Deva
Whatever the name, whatever the form, we know You’ve come to save us
Sai Ram, Sai Ram, let us feel Your Love and Grace
Sai Ram, Bhagawan, let us see You in every face
 
Sathya Sai Bhagawan
Sarva dharma priya Deva
We know that You’ve come to teach us how to love
You’ve come to teach us seva
Sai Ram, Sai Ram, when we practice what You teach
It is then that we know that we’re on the right path
We’ll all meet at Your feet
