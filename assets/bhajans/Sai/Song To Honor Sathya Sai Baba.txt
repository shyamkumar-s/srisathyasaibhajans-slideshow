Song To Honor Sathya Sai Baba
Sathya Sai, Poorna Avatar, known as Shirdi Sai a long time ago,
Divine vibhuti flows from His hand, bringing peace to all man.
The Godly glance fills all hearts, as His form silently speaks,
And once again He will appear, as Prema Sai Avatar.
Sathya Sai, Poorna Avatar (4x)
