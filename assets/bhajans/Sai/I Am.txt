I Am
I Am the mountains, I Am the plains; I Am the desert, I Am the rains
I Am the ocean, I Am the shore; I Am all of these, and infinitely more
I AM THAT, I AM THAT, I AM THAT….THAT I AM
 
I Am the rivers, I Am the streams; I Am the moonlight, and the sun’s beams
I Am You, I Am Me; I Am all that ever can be
I Am the bird, I Am the beast; I Am the greatest, I Am the least
I AM THAT, I AM THAT, I AM THAT….THAT I AM
 
I Am the night, I Am the day; I Am the Light, I Am The Way
I Am the lion, I Am the lamb; throughout My Kingdom, I AM THAT I AM
I Am the valley, I Am the sky; I Am the secret place of The Most High
I AM THAT, I AM THAT, I AM THAT….THAT I AM
 
I Am the moon, I Am the sun; I Am the many, I Am The One
I Am the water, I Am the wine; I Am the branch at One with the vine
I Am the stone that David hurled; I Am the Kingdom not of this world
I AM THAT, I AM THAT, I AM THAT….THAT I AM
 
I Am the bread, I am the meat; closer than breathing or hands and feet
I Am the rocks, I Am the sands; I Am the temple not made with human hands
I Am the sounds that can be heard; I Am the Truth, I AM THE WORD
I AM THAT, I AM THAT, I AM THAT….THAT I AM

I Am the minute, I Am the hour; I Am Omnipotence, The One and Only Power
I Am the people of every race; I Am Love and I Am Grace
I Am the dark, I Am the fair; I Am communion of an answered prayer
I AM THAT, I AM THAT, I AM THAT….THAT I AM
 
I AM the staff, I Am the rod; BE STILL AND KNOW I AM GOD
