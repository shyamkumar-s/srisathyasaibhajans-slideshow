Abide In The Word
As the branch cannot bear fruit of itself
Except it abide in the Vine
No more can ye unless you abide in Me
So that I might abide in You.
 
Every tree that exists simply consists
Of the Presence of God I Am
It shows as it grows that it’s part of the whole
As it glorifies its Father within..
