Sathya Sai, I Sing
And the beating of my heart is Your love, is Your love,
And the beating of my heart is Your love,
And the breathing in and out is Your grace, is Your grace,
And the breathing in and out is Your grace.
Sathya Sai, I sing, sing, Sathya Sai, I sing, sing,
In the silence of my mind is Your voice, is Your voice,
In the silence of my mind is Your voice,
And the yearning of my soul is Your name, is Your name,
And the yearning of my soul is Your name.
