In the realm
Sathya Sai Baba, our guiding light, 
Your presence transcends, divine and pure,
Channeling psychic strengths so sure.

Across East African lands, Your love does spread,
Shielding us from fears, strange diseases dread.
In gratitude, we raise our voice, 
For gifts of gold and treasures choice.

Oh Baba, Your blessings endless flow, 
In every prayer, in every glow, 
Forever in our hearts, You stay, 
Leading us on the righteous way.

In whispers soft or chants profound, 
Your wisdom echoes all around.
With faith, we walk, with love we pray, 
Sathya Sai Baba, our guiding ray.

In moments dark, in times of need, 
Your grace uplifts us, indeed.
Oh Baba, eternally we sing,
Of the peace Your teachings bring.

Divine healer, guardian true, 
In our hearts, we honor You.
For in Your presence, we find solace
Sathya Sai Baba, forever we declare
