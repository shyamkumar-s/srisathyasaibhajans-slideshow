Happy Birthday, My Baba
May Your smile be the smile that I wore on the day I fell in love with You,
Each day is a joyous new birthday, each day is another new start,
For each day when I rise and open my eyes, I light the candle of love in my heart.
And today as I work in my garden, won’t You walk by my side as I go,
‘Til the daylight is gone and my work is all done, and together we tend the last row.

I remember the day I first met You, and that day saw my purpose fulfilled,
When love had begun and two became one, I surrender to Your will.
May the moonlight fall soft on Your pillow tonight, may Your dreams be of peace and love,
‘Til the morning dawns bright at the end of the night, may You dance through the heavens above.

So, happy birthday, my/Sai Baba, may this day see Your purpose fulfilled,
May love’s new rebirth bring peace on the earth and surrender to Your will.
May the moonlight fall soft on Your pillow tonight, may Your dreams be of peace and love,
‘Til the morning dawns bright at the end of the night, may You dance through the heavens above.
