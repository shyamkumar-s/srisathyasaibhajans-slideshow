Riversong
The river is rain and the river is snow,
The river is high and the river is low,
It’ll take you to the sea. (4x)

Oh, riversong, singing to me, telling me, “you are of the sea”,
Oh, riversong, singing to me, telling me, “you will make it to the sea”.
You will make it to the sea. (4x)

From the mountain up high to the valley below,
The rain will come and the rain will go,
The river is high, and the river is low,
It’ll take you to the sea.
It’ll take you to the sea. (4x)

Oh, oh, oh, listen to the riversong, oh oh, oh, calling to me,
Oh, oh, oh, can’t you hear the riversong, oh singing of the sea.

The sun is shining on the mountaintop,
The snow is melting, and it’s drop by drop,
It’s starting to trickle, and it’s starting to flow,
And other are coming, and it’s starting to grow,
And it’s tumbling through the forest and beginning its song,
It sings out louder and it’s rushing along,
It sings about heaven, and it sings about rain,
It sings about pleasure, and it sings about pain,
It sings about the mountain, and it sings about the sun,
How many come together, and they’re flowing as one,
It sings about you, and it sings about me,
And it’s rushing to the sea.

Come, come, come, come and sing the riversong,
Come, come, come, come along with me,
Come, come, come, come and sing the riversong,
Oh, sailing to the sea.
Riversong, take me to the sea, (2x)
Oooh, take me to the sea.
