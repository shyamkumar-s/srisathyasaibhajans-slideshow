Utter The Name
Rama, Krishna, Hari, Rama, Krishna, Hari
Use as a mantra, Tuka says, Rama, Krishna, Hari
Over and over fervently, Rama, Krishna, Hari
Rama, Krishna, Hari, Rama, Krishna, Hari
 
Tuka says that God will run to where His name is sung
If you follow faithfully, Rama, Krishna, Hari
God will grant the golden key to His secret treasury
