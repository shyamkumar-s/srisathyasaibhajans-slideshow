Kanaka Wai Wai
Ma ke ala hele o Jesu
(In the travels of Jesus)
I halawai aku ai
(He met)
Me ke kanaka opio hanohano
(A prominent young man)
Kaulana e ka wai wai
(Known for his great wealth)
 
Pane mai e ka opio.
(Said the youth)
E ku’u haku mai ka’i
(My good Lord)
He aha ho’i ka’u e hana aku ai
(What must I do)
I loa’a e ke ola mau?
(To have life eternal?)
 
E ha’awi, e ha’awi lilo
(Give…give away)
I kou mau wai wai
(All your possessions)
Huli a hahai mai ia’u
(Turn and follow Me)
I loa’a e ke ola mau (ia oe)
(Then you will have eternal life)
